Rules:

  • The game begins with the player's character at the maze's starting position.
  • Players can move their character using arrow keys.
  • The logo of digital India appears 10 times at different places throughout the maze.
  • When the player touches the logo, a question is raised and 10 points are added for every correct answer
  • Otherwise, 10 points are deducted for each wrong answer.
  • To win the game, player must score more than 50 points.
  • The game continues until the player collects all points.
  • The game ends with a victory message if the desired criteria is reached.
